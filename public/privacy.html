Privacy Policy – Zewelry

Last updated: 06/03/2025

At Zewelry, we take your privacy very seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user.

1. Who is responsible for processing your data?

  • Responsible: Zewelry
  • Contact email: zewelry.contact@gmail.com
  • Location: Spain

2. What data do we collect?

  • Browsing data (IP, browser type, language, etc.).
  • Usage data (pages visited, clicks, time on the website).
  • Purchase data (name, email, address, products, payment method).
  • Data provided through forms (e.g., contact, subscription).

3. What do we use your data for?

  • To manage your orders and payments.
  • To send you confirmation, tracking, or support emails.
  • To analyze how you use the website (only if you accept cookies).
  • To comply with legal obligations (invoicing, returns, etc.).

4. What is our legal basis for processing your data?

  • Your consent (when you accept cookies or subscribe).
  • Contract (to process your orders).
  • Legal obligation (for example, to keep invoices).
  • Legitimate interest (to improve our website and products).

5. Who do we share your data with?

We only share data with providers who need it to operate:

  • Stripe or PayPal (payment processing).
  • Google Analytics (if you enable analytics cookies).
  • Shipping platforms (if we offer physical delivery).

We never sell or rent your data to third parties.

6. What are your rights?

You can exercise the following rights:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Rectify it if it is incorrect.
  • Request its deletion.
  • Limit its use.
  • Withdraw your consent at any time.

To do so, contact us at zewelry.contact@gmail.com indicating the right you wish to exercise.

7. How long do we keep your data?

  • Order data: 5 years (by tax law).
  • Browsing data: 1 year or until you delete cookies.
  • Contact emails: up to 12 months.

8. Cookies

We use our own and third-party cookies to:

  • Function correctly (technical cookies).
  • Measure website usage (only if you accept them).
  • Remember your preferences.

More information in our Cookies Policy.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when necessary. We will notify you if the changes are significant.

10. Questions?

You can write to us at zewelry.contact@gmail.com and we will reply as soon as possible.
